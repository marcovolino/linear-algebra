Use one brief as a capstone or combine two into a larger portfolio project.

## SVD Image Compression

Load a greyscale image, reconstruct it at several ranks, and compare storage, relative error, and visual quality. Deliver a notebook or script, three reconstructions, and a short discussion of the singular-value spectrum.

## PCA Feature Explorer

Centre an image or feature dataset, compute principal components with SVD, and visualise samples in two dimensions. Report explained variance and discuss what the leading directions capture.

## Transformation Engine

Build a NumPy tool that composes translation, rotation, scale, and shear in homogeneous coordinates. Transform a point set and demonstrate how changing operation order changes the result.

## Camera Projection Simulator

Create 3D points, define intrinsic and extrinsic camera matrices, and project the points into an image. Include depth handling, coordinate conventions, and at least two camera poses.

## Least-Squares Calibration

Estimate an affine or camera-related model from noisy correspondences. Report matrix rank, condition number, residual RMSE, and hold-out error, then explain any weakly constrained parameters.
