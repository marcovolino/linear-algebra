These exercises support the introduction and vector lessons.

## Concept Questions

1. Give an example of a scalar, vector, and matrix in a computer vision task.
2. Explain why a vector can represent either a geometric arrow or a list of features.
3. Describe one way a matrix can represent an image.

## Calculation Questions

1. Compute $\lVert [3,4]^{\mathsf T} \rVert_2$.
2. Compute $[1,2,3]^{\mathsf T} + [4,0,-1]^{\mathsf T}$.
3. Find the unit vector in the direction of $[0,5]^{\mathsf T}$.
