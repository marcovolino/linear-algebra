## Concept Questions

1. What makes an eigenvector direction invariant?
2. Why does the characteristic equation find eigenvalues?
3. When is a matrix diagonalizable?
4. How do eigenvalue magnitudes determine stability?
5. What special guarantees hold for real symmetric matrices?

## Calculation Questions

1. Find the eigenpairs of $\begin{bmatrix}3&0\\0&-2\end{bmatrix}$.
2. Compute the characteristic polynomial of $\begin{bmatrix}4&1\\2&3\end{bmatrix}$.
3. Diagonalise that matrix.
4. Use diagonalisation to compute its fifth power.
5. Find the spectral radius of a matrix with eigenvalues $0.4$, $-0.8$, and $1.2$.

## Coding Questions

1. Compute and verify eigenpairs with `np.linalg.eig`.
2. Reconstruct a diagonalizable matrix from $PDP^{-1}$.
3. Simulate a discrete system for several initial states.
4. Orthogonally diagonalise a symmetric matrix with `np.linalg.eigh`.
