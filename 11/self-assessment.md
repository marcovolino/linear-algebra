## Question 2
Why does PCA centre data?
<details><summary>Show solution</summary>So components describe variation around the mean rather than the mean's offset from the origin.</details>

## Question 3
What does cosine similarity ignore?
<details><summary>Show solution</summary>It ignores overall non-zero vector magnitude and compares direction.</details>
